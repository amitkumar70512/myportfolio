About Me

My Story

I'm an Android Developer with 2+ years of experience at DISH Network Technologies, specializing in media streaming, DVR playback, and player optimization for OTT platforms. I've led critical migrations from ExoPlayer to Media3, reducing video startup time (VST) by 2-3 seconds, and implemented DSMCC-based offline playback that drove higher sales for offline-demanding partners.

I hold a Bachelor's degree in Computer Science from B.M.S College of Engineering, Bangalore (Grade: 8.7/10). My expertise spans Kotlin, Java, Jetpack Compose, Media3, ExoPlayer, Bitmovin Player, and modern Android architecture patterns (MVVM, Clean Architecture). When I'm not coding, you'll find me reading about mythology, tending to my plants, staying fit, or exploring photography.

My Approach

I believe in writing clean, maintainable code that scales. My development philosophy centers around user-centric design, performance optimization, and continuous learning. I've introduced KTLint rules for code consistency, performed peer code reviews, and fixed critical crashes to ensure robust, production-ready applications.
